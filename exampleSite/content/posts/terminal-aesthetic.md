Why build a website that looks like a terminal?

## Nostalgia

There's something satisfying about the simplicity of text interfaces. No distractions, just content.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." - Leonardo da Vinci

## Speed

Keyboard nav feels snappy and fun.
