The Terminal Aesthetic

January 13, 2025 #design #tui

Why build a website that looks like a terminal?

Nostalgia

There’s something satisfying about the simplicity of text interfaces. No distractions, just content.

Focus

When everything is keyboard-driven, users are forced to slow down and actually read. No infinite scrolling, no algorithmic feeds.

Performance

No web fonts to load. No heavy JavaScript frameworks. Just HTML, CSS, and a bit of vanilla JS.

“Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.” - Leonardo da Vinci

The Cursor

The blinking green cursor is iconic. It says “I’m ready. What’s next?”

█

That’s all you need.
